Due to the coming into force of the 370 Act, the state of Jammu and Kashmir was divided into two union territories. First Jammu and second Ladakh.

Due to the implementation of the 370 Act, the State of Jammu and Kashmir was divided into two Unitary Territories. First Jammu and the second Ladakh


Before Article 370 - The J&K state had two different flags - India and the state.

After Article 370 - There would be no separate flag for the state.
